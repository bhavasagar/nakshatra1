3 minutes 1 issue, 2 minutes and 30 seconds to order, 30 seconds to show the lottery result. It opens all day. The total number of trade is 480 issues


If you spend 100 to trade, after deducting 3 service fee, your contract amount is 97:

1. JOIN GREEN: If the result shows 1,3,7,9, you will get (100*2) 197 If the result shows 5, you will get (100*1.5) 147

2. JOIN RED: If the result shows 2,4,6,8, you will get (100*2) 197; If the result shows 0, you will get (100*1.5) 147

3. JOIN PURPLE: If the result shows 0 or 5, you will get (100*2) 197.

4. SELECT NUMBER: If the result is the same as the number you selected, you will get (100*7) 679.
